Porsche 911 Turbo S



The Porsche 911 Turbo S is the ultimate expression of precision engineering and everyday supercar performance. Renowned for its perfect blend of luxury, speed, and practicality, the Turbo S sits at the very top of the 911 lineup, delivering a driving experience that few cars in the world can match. Beneath its iconic, timeless silhouette lies a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged flat-six engine that produces an exhilarating 640 horsepower and 800 Nm of torque, launching the car from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.7 seconds. Thanks to its intelligent all-wheel-drive system and lightning-fast PDK transmission, the Turbo S offers relentless acceleration and incredible traction, even in challenging conditions. But the 911 Turbo S is not just about straight-line speed; it’s a masterclass in control and balance. With rear-axle steering, active aerodynamics, and adaptive suspension, it carves through corners with extraordinary agility while maintaining the comfort and refinement Porsche is known for. The exterior design is both elegant and aggressive, featuring wider fenders, active rear spoilers, and distinctive Turbo-exclusive details that hint at the car’s immense capabilities. Inside, the cabin is a seamless blend of tradition and innovation. Luxurious materials, cutting-edge technology, and impeccable ergonomics create a space that feels equally suited for daily commutes or weekend track days. The Porsche Advanced Cockpit features a crisp digital display combined with classic analog elements, keeping the spirit of the 911 alive while embracing the future. The 911 Turbo S is not just a supercar — it’s a symbol of evolution, representing over five decades of relentless improvement, passion, and precision. It’s an icon that refuses to compromise, offering the thrill of ultimate performance without sacrificing everyday usability.
