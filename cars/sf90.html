Ferrari SF 90 Stradale



The Ferrari SF90 Stradale stands at the cutting edge of automotive innovation, blending traditional Italian craftsmanship with the future of hybrid performance. As Ferrari’s first production plug-in hybrid supercar, the SF90 Stradale delivers an astonishing combination of raw power, electric efficiency, and advanced technology. Under its sculpted hood lies a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, paired with three electric motors, generating a combined total of 986 horsepower. This immense power enables the SF90 Stradale to sprint from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.5 seconds, making it one of the quickest road cars ever built. Beyond its breathtaking performance, the SF90 Stradale showcases Ferrari’s relentless focus on design and aerodynamics. Its aggressive, low-slung stance, sharp lines, and intricate detailing reflect both beauty and function, improving downforce and cooling at high speeds. Inside, the cockpit is a perfect fusion of luxury and race-inspired minimalism, featuring a fully digital interface, touch-sensitive controls, and premium materials throughout. The car also offers several driving modes — including a pure electric eDrive mode — giving drivers the flexibility to cruise silently or unleash the full fury of its hybrid system. The SF90 Stradale is not just a leap forward for Ferrari; it's a statement of what the future of supercars looks like — bold, electrified, and uncompromisingly thrilling.
